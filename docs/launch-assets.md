# Launch & Marketing Strategy

This guide provides a roadmap for launching BusinessOps Suite and reaching your first 100 users.

## 1. Social Media Assets

### Launch Post (Twitter/X & LinkedIn)
**Hook:** Stop juggling 10 different business tools. 🚀
**Body:** We just built BusinessOps Suite — the all-in-one platform for modern teams.
- Project Management ✅
- Team Collaboration ✅
- Payments & Invoicing ✅
- AI Business Assistant ✅
**CTA:** Get started for free: [Your Link Here]
#SaaS #BusinessOps #Productivity #BuildInPublic

### Images to Prepare
- **Hero Image:** High-quality screenshot of the dashboard.
- **Feature Cards:** Individual graphics for Project Management, AI Assistant, and Payments.
- **OG Image:** 1200x630 image (already configured in `layout.tsx`).

## 2. Platform Specific Launches

### Product Hunt
- **Tagline:** The unified OS for your business.
- **Description:** A short, punchy paragraph explaining the "Why".
- **Gallery:** 5-7 screenshots showing off the premium UI.
- **Maker's Comment:** Tell the story of why you built this.

## 3. SEO Checklist (Post-Deployment)
- [ ] Connect to Google Search Console.
- [ ] Submit `sitemap.xml` (located at `/sitemap`).
- [ ] Verify `robots.txt` is accessible.
- [ ] Run a PageSpeed Insights test.

## 4. Community Outreach
- Post in Reddit communities like `r/SaaS`, `r/Entrepreneur`, `r/SmallBusiness`.
- Share with friends and family for initial feedback and support.
- Email your existing list if you have one.
